Pixel Eyes · AI Knock Engine

A knock you feel.

Pro models add a pixel-eye display that blinks, smiles and reacts — giving your Pocknoq a little personality. Behind it, an on-device knock engine filters the noise and decides what truly matters, then delivers it as a clean, expressive knock in your palm with a soft glow and a familiar voice.
